About Us

What is Follow Up?

Follow Up is directly oriented to facilitate the checking and data collection for new applicants to ¡Supérate! scholarship.

Follow up is created to have the proper order of the information of each student, in the application those in charge of the selection process will have an account in which they can create the profiles of the different applicants and enter their most important information such as their name and how to contact them at the same time having a check list for each student and mark the process of each of them, the application has a chat bot in which those in charge of the selection process can say which institutions they visited and thus creating a list.


Follow Up and ¡Supérate!

How did the idea of Follow Up come up?


Follow Up was created with the intention of helping all the ¡Supérate! centers to keep track of the data of all those young people who are in the selection process.



How does this App help ¡Supérate! ?


Follow Up is oriented to facilitate and verify the information collected from new applicants.




What is ¡Supérate!?






¡Supérate! provides high quality education in English, ICT and values to young people of high academic performance for three years. The young people receive three hours a day of classes, complementary and parallel to the public education system.





At the end of the program, they obtain international certifications that guarantee an advanced command of the English language and the efficient use of Microsoft Office and programming tools.





The teenagers also have a solid foundation of values and reflect a commitment to continue contributing to the country's socioeconomic development.

If you want to know more about ¡Supérate! and you are interested in participating in the selection process or becoming part of this excellent adventure; these are the means where you can get in touch with ¡Supérate! and be aware of the important dates of the selection process.
